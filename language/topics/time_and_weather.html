                                                # Time & Weather & seasons

                                                # 1. English

                                                # 1.1. Time

                                                Season: 
spring, summer, autumn/fall and winter

the days of the week: 
Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday

the months of the year: 
January, February, March, 
April ([eI]), May, June, 
July, August, September,
October, Novermber, December

                                                ⚠︎ Note: The days of the week and months of the year always have a capital letter.

                                                What time is it?
Today is Sunday.
My birthday is in October.
My favourite day is Saturday.
Summer is my favorite season.
I can't wait until fall.
Winters here are freezing.
I will come back in spring.

                                                # 1.2. Weather

                                                Weather

                                                • Sunny / Clear
                                                • Cloudy / Overcast
                                                • Rainy / Raining
                                                • Stormy / Storming
                                                • Hailing
                                                • Snowy / Snowing
                                                It's raining.
It's sunny outside.
It's an overcast day.
The weather is rainy today.

                                                Temperature

                                                • Nice
                                                • 70 degrees
                                                • Warm
                                                • Hot
                                                • Boiling
                                                • Cool
                                                • Cold
                                                • Freezing
                                                It's nice outside.
It's boiling!
The weather is cool today.
It will be 65 degrees tomorrow.

                                                Conversations:

                                                What's the weather like?
It's raining.
Great! I love rainy weather.
I don't! I love clear days.

What's it like outside?
It's clear, but it's super hot.
Summers here are miserable.
But it cools off in the evenings.
